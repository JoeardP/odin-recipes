Sinigang na Baboy

Sinigang na baboy is a tender pork sour soup mixed with vegetables and best served with spicy Fish Sauce as dip

Ingredients

  • Pork Belly
  • Tomato
  • Water Spinach
  • Okra
  • Bean String
  • Tamarind Puree

Steps

  1. Boil Pork Belly in water until tender
  2. Remove scum on surface
  3. Put tamarind puree and taste until it reached your preferred taste
  4. Mix vegetables
  5. Serve with spicy fish sauce
